Pinto Beans

Known for their creamy texture and earthy taste, pinto beans are versatile and can be used in everything from chili to burritos. They are a great source of fiber and nutrients.

Kidney Beans

These vibrant red beans are not only visually appealing but also nutritious. Ideal for stews, salads, and casseroles, kidney beans add a hearty touch to any dish.

Navy Beans

Small and white, navy beans are perfect for making classic dishes like baked beans and soups. They are high in fiber and low in fat, making them a healthy choice for any meal.

Chickpeas

Also known as garbanzo beans, chickpeas are incredibly versatile. Use them in salads, curries, or to make delicious hummus. They are rich in protein and essential nutrients.

Lentils

Our lentils are available in various colors, including green, red, and brown. They cook quickly and are perfect for soups, salads, and side dishes. High in protein and fiber, lentils are a fantastic addition to any diet.
